# TV Time Out by Refract

A Chrome extension that exports your complete TV Time tracking data
directly from your browser — no server, no account required.

## What it does

- Exports your full library: tracked series and anime (with seasons,
  episodes, watch status, watched dates, and watch counts), favorites,
  movies, and custom lists.
- Output formats: JSON, CSV, or both. Optional ZIP bundle for a single
  download.
- Includes a visual HTML summary with sortable, filterable columns,
  plus a failed-export log so nothing is silently dropped.

## How to use

1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
2. Open [app.tvtime.com](https://app.tvtime.com) and log in.
3. Click the TV Time Out icon in your Chrome toolbar.
4. Pick a format and hit **Export my data**.

Your files are saved straight to your device. No data ever leaves your
browser.

## Privacy

100% client-side. All processing happens locally; nothing is uploaded
anywhere. No account, no server, no tracking.
