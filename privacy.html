TV Time Out — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

No data collection

TV Time Out does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser. No information ever leaves your device.

How it works

The extension reads your TV Time authentication token and user ID directly from your browser's local storage while you are logged in to tvtime.com. It uses these credentials to fetch your data from the TV Time API on your behalf, then saves the result as JSON files on your device.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • scripting — required to read authentication tokens from the tvtime.com page context due to CORS restrictions.
  • tabs — required to identify the active tvtime.com tab for script injection.
  • downloads — required to save the exported JSON files to your device.
